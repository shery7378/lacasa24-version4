Description

Set above the lush Ubud ridge, this private hillside villa combines modern tropical architecture with high-end finishes designed for long stays and international tenants. The home features an open-concept living room with floor-to-ceiling glass doors, a chef-style kitchen with premium appliances, and a 20-meter infinity pool overlooking uninterrupted jungle views.

Each bedroom has an ensuite bathroom, custom storage, and dedicated climate control. The master suite includes a deep soaking tub and private terrace. Reliable high-speed fiber internet, a backup power system, and weekly maintenance make this property ideal for remote professionals, relocating families, or executive rentals.

The villa is located in a secure gated lane 10 minutes from central Ubud and within easy reach of wellness studios, international schools, cafes, and medical services.
